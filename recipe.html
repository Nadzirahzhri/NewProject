Burnt Cheesecake Recipe!

  • 250 gram cream cheese
  • 2 Grade A eggs
  • 200 grams of whipping cream / thick cream
  • 100 grams of caster sugar
  • 15grams of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 1/4 teaspoon of lemon juice (optional)


Instruction

  1. 1. Add cream cheese, whipping cream, sugar, eggs, flour, and vanilla essence into a blender. Blend all the ingredients until smooth.
  2. 2. Prepare a 6" cake tin lined with baking paper. Pour the cream cheese mixture into the tin. Bake in the air fryer at 180°C-230°C for 25-30 minutes.
  3. 3. Once cooled, store the cake in the chiller for one hour. Then, remove it from the tin to slice and it's ready to serve. You can also sprinkle snow powder if you like!
